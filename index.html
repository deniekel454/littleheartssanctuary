Our Challenges

Running an orphanage comes with many challenges. We often face difficulties such as:

  • Limited funding for daily necessities and school fees
  • Inadequate medical supplies and healthcare access
  • Shortage of proper clothing and bedding for the children
  • Insufficient infrastructure and maintenance needs

Despite these hardships, we remain committed to our mission through the generous support of well-wishers and sponsors.

Financial Accountability

At Little Hearts Sanctuary, we value transparency and integrity. All donations are carefully recorded and used strictly for the well-being of our children and the improvement of our facilities.

How To Help

You can support Little Hearts Sanctuary in several ways:

  • Donate Funds: Your financial support helps us cover food, healthcare, education, and maintenance costs.
  • Volunteer Your Time: Assist with education, events, or daily care activities.
  • Provide Supplies: Clothing, school supplies, books, and hygiene products are always needed.
  • Spread Awareness: Share our mission and stories on social media to reach more supporters.
